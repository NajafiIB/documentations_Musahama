# Results Workflow

Owner: Platform Architect
Last Updated: 2026-03-18
Version: 1.0
Status: Approved

---

## 1. Purpose

Define how candidate outputs are reviewed, acted on, and turned into useful final records.

---

## 2. Role in the Product

Results is the review and action stage of the workflow.

It is where the user:
- reviews candidates
- inspects supporting data
- sees fit/analysis outputs
- shortlists
- exports
- opens canonical company/contact records

Results is not the final canonical archive itself.

---

## 3. Main Flow

### Step 1 — Receive candidate results
Read results generated by research and strategies.

### Step 2 — Resolve linked entities
Where available, link or promote data to:
- companies
- contacts

### Step 3 — Resolve normalized analysis
Load:
- evidence
- psych_profiles
- lmc_fits
- dossiers

### Step 4 — Review result detail
Inspect:
- company summary
- contact summary
- evidence
- fit analysis
- dossier summary

### Step 5 — Act on result
Allowed actions may include:
- shortlist
- export dossier
- export CSV/PDF
- open linked company
- open linked contact

---

### Result detail
- inspector/detail panel
- canonical company summary
- canonical contact summary

- fit analysis

---

## 5. Important Product Rules

### shortlist
Shortlist is a results action or subview.
It is not a top-level module.

### dossier
Dossier is a results detail/export behavior.
It is not a top-level module.

### discovery
Discovery is not a top-level module.
Discovery-like behavior belongs inside research/results workflow.

---

## 6. Data Touchpoints

Workflow input tables:
- research_results

- companies

- contacts
- contact_emails
- contact_phones

Analysis tables:
- evidence

- lmc_fits
- dossiers

---

## 7. Source-of-Truth Rule

Final result detail UI must prefer normalized data.

Use:
- canonical company/contact tables
- normalized analysis tables

Do not make final UI depend primarily on:
- research_results.company_profile
- research_results.psych_profile
- research_results.lmc_fit
- research_results.dossier
- research_results.evidence

Those are transitional compatibility fields only.

---

## 8. Capability Examples

Typical feature keys:
- results.shortlist
- results.export.csv
- results.export.dossier
- results.export.pdf
- results.compare_candidates

These capabilities must still be resolved through the feature-system.

---

## 9. Hard Rules

1. results review must be separated from research execution detail
2. final entity/archive pages must not be built from raw result blobs
3. shortlist and export are actions, not modules
4. result detail should point users toward canonical company/contact records where available
5. disabled actions must reflect runtime reason codes, not random UI guesses

---

## 10. Output of This Workflow Stage

The output is:
- reviewed results
- shortlist/export actions
- linked or promoted canonical records
- normalized analysis outputs used elsewhere in the app
