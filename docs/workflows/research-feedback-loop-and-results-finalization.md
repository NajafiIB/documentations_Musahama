# Research Feedback Loop and Results Finalization

Owner: Platform Architect
Last Updated: 2026-03-27
Version: 1.0
Status: Approved

## Purpose

Define the workflow split between Research and Results.

## Research owns
- strategy
- positive signals
- constraints
- discovery
- shortlist decision capture
- reject decision capture
- refinement proposals

## Results owns
- shortlisted cases
- finalized review
- evidence detail
- fit review
- dossier review
- export
- downstream submission

## Required feedback loop
1. candidate appears in Discovery
2. operator reviews it
3. operator chooses shortlist, reject, or no action
4. system may suggest a reusable rule
5. operator may approve, edit, or dismiss it
6. approved rules affect future search behavior

## Rule types
- positive signals
- constraints

## Scope
- category
- global

## Decision outcomes
### Shortlist
- candidate leaves discovery working set
- candidate becomes eligible for Results
- system may suggest a positive signal

### Reject
- candidate leaves discovery working set or moves to archive
- system may suggest a constraint

### No action
- candidate stays in discovery review state

## Final rule
Research is for search and learning.
Results is for shortlisted and finalized review.
