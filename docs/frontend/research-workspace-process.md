# Research Workspace Process

Owner: Platform Architect
Last Updated: 2026-03-27
Version: 1.0
Status: Approved

## Purpose

Describe the operator-facing process for the Research workspace and the handoff to Results.

## Target flow
1. Strategy
2. Positive Signals
3. Constraints
4. Discovery
5. Results

## Research page layout
The Research page should contain:
- stable shell
- mandate summary
- active track summary
- counters for discovered, shortlisted, and rejected items
- clear stage switcher

## Stage switcher
Use these labels:
- Strategy
- Positive Signals
- Constraints
- Discovery

## Stage meanings
### Strategy
- mandate definition
- category setup
- source planning
- search setup

### Positive Signals
- preferred target qualities
- good match examples
- approved positive rules

### Constraints
- exclusions
- disqualifiers
- approved negative rules

### Discovery
- candidate review
- evidence review
- shortlist and reject actions
- learning proposals

## Decision panel
After shortlist or reject, the UI should allow:
- decision reason
- suggested rule text
- rule type
- scope
- approve
- edit and approve
- dismiss

## Results page
The Results page should focus on:
- shortlisted cases
- finalized review
- evidence detail
- fit detail
- dossier detail
- export
- downstream submission

## Final rule
Research is for search and learning.
Results is for shortlisted and finalized cases.
